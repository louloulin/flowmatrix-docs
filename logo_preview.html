Logo Description

The FlowMatrix logo represents the dual nature of the platform:

  • Flow: The flowing, wave-like elements represent streaming data processing, showing the continuous flow of information.
  • Matrix: The grid background represents structured data and batch processing capabilities, symbolizing organization and analysis.

The color palette uses shades of blue to convey trust, reliability, and data intelligence. The modern, clean typography emphasizes the professional and technical nature of the platform.
